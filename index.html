About us

A Vibes Events Co. proudly announce that company got the approval to show movies at Brooklyn Bridge Park at this year "Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival" from August 5th through 8th, from 6 pm to midnight. We can't wait to meet the public and to discover excellent films together!

Shows will be free and open to everyone, but we ask you to please participate in pre-registration so that we can estimate how many people might come and so that we can close registration if it reaches the venue’s capacity of 5,000 people, which we hope it will.

We hope selection of films this year is excellent. From August 5th to 8th at Brooklyn Bridge Park will be fantastic show. Also, we wanted with this year selection to create an event that shows that films can appeal to audiences of all ages. On the list are two animated new films so bring your kids. Both screenings are early in the afternoon.

Pre-register

We wish everyone to come and watch films with us, but that will maybe not be possible. This venue is limited to 5000 seats, and we need to find out if we need to issue tickets for our visitors. With a reservation, everyone who pre-registered will have his place reserved. So, please, we ask you to pre-register your seat while it's available.

We will issue tickets for every day of the festival for every registered person for free. Every ticket holder can come from 5 pm and stay till midnight enjoying in selected films.

Please, select the number of persons that will come with you next to the day you plan to come (including yourself). Valid email address is required for validation of your registration, and only one registration per email address is possible.

Tip: If you only want to send a message then you don't need to select days or how many people are coming. Just send the word, and we will answer it as soon as possible.
