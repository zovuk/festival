About us

A Vibes Events Co. proudly announce that company got the approval to show movies at Brooklyn Bridge Park at this year "Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival" from August 5th through 8th, from 6 pm to midnight. We can't wait to meet the public and to discover awesome films together!

Shows will be free and open to everyone, but we ask you to please participate in pre-registration so that we are able to estimate how many people might come and so that we can close registration if it reaches the venue’s capacity of 5,000 people. Which we hope it will.

We hope to select and show only your favorite films from August 5th to 8th at Brooklyn Bridge Park. Also, we want to create an event that shows that films can appeal to audiences of all ages. Please pre-registrate with possible dates of your participation in this event and please do seggest what movies you think should be played.
